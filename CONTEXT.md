# DazDotDev Website

This context defines the language used to describe Darren Barklie's public positioning on dazdotdev.com.

## Language

**Technical Founder**:
A person who builds both businesses and the technology platforms those businesses depend on. This is the primary identity for Darren's current positioning.
_Avoid_: Full stack developer, agency owner, AI expert

**Notes**:
The public writing section for observations, self-reflection, technical learning, and unfinished thinking. Notes are written primarily to sharpen Darren's own judgment rather than to perform content marketing.
_Avoid_: Blog, content, SEO writing, bytes

**Learning in Public**:
A plainspoken practice of publishing observations while Darren's thinking is still developing. It should read as self-accountability and reflection, not guru positioning.
_Avoid_: Thought leadership, personal brand, content strategy

**AI-Assisted Software Work**:
Software and platform work shaped by current AI development practices, data, and machine learning. The phrase should signal direction and practical curiosity without overclaiming AI expertise.
_Avoid_: AI-native, AI expert, AI bro, leveraging AI
